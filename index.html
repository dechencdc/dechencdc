About Dechen

Dechen Child & Adults Rehabilitation Centre is a dedicated facility providing comprehensive rehabilitation and therapeutic services for children and adults. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering personalized care tailored to each individual's unique needs.

We specialize in early interventions, behavioral therapy, speech therapy, occupational therapy, and comprehensive psychological assessments. Our holistic approach ensures optimal outcomes for our clients across all age groups.
